AI Vision Studio

AI Vision Studio is a dual-purpose web application that transforms images through artificial intelligence. Using advanced machine learning models, it converts visual input into either expressive poetry or specialized plant care advice, offering users both creative inspiration and practical guidance in a seamless experience.

Milestone 1: Poetry Vision

Through advanced AI technology, your visual inspirations are transformed into elegant verse, combining image recognition and creative language models to craft unique poems that capture the essence of each moment. This system serves as your personal poetry companion, turning everyday scenes into artistic expressions.

Utilizing Hugging Face's advanced model to interpret uploaded images, the system then leverages OpenAI's API to craft original poems inspired by visual elements. Your poetry is elegantly presented alongside the original image, creating an immersive visual-verbal experience, all built with modern web technologies for an intuitive and responsive interface.

Milestone 2: Garden Fairy

Garden Sprite is an AI-powered companion that combines poetic creativity with plant care expertise. Through advanced machine learning, it transforms plant images into either expressive poetry or professional care guidance, serving as both your artistic muse and knowledgeable garden assistant.

Using a specialized RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) database, the system provides expert care recommendations based on your plant photos. Users can choose from various care aspects like light, watering, and temperature to receive targeted advice. The application delivers specific, actionable guidance while adapting its recommendations to different plant types and environmental conditions, making plant care both accessible and professional.
